Biography

Born in Niš (Serbia), in 1989. Finished high artistic school in 2008, after which she enrolled basic academic studies at the Faculty of Arts, in the Department of painting and graduates in 2014.
- Master studies completed in 2015. at the same University in the class of Professor Mr. Katarina Djordjevic.
Had six solo exhibitions, and participated in over 60. group exhibitions in her country and abroad.
- Winner of the award for second place for graphics at the "Fifth International Biennale of Artistic Drawing and Graphics", "Dušan Starčević" Memorial Gallery.
- Winner of the first prize at the exhibition "Fortress through the lens of contemporaries - then and now" SLU Gallery in Niš, 2023.

- In 2024. got the degree in Internet Marketing in Level Up school for programing.
